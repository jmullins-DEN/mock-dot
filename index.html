RATING THRESHOLDS (49 CFR § 385.5)

SATISFACTORY — No acute violations; no pattern of critical violations. Factor score ≥ 88/99 per factor.

CONDITIONAL — Has critical or acute violations; factor scores between 50–87.

UNSATISFACTORY — Multiple acute violations OR factor score below 50.

Tie-Breaker Score = (1 − violations/total) × 100
Acute violations automatically result in Unsatisfactory regardless of factor scores. Official acute violations include: § 382.115(a) no testing program, § 382.305 no random testing, § 382.211 driver who refused test, § 382.503 no follow-up tests, § 383.37(a) suspended/revoked CDL, § 383.37(b) multiple licenses, § 391.11 unqualified driver, § 391.51 no DQ file, § 395.8 no RODS/false logs, § 395.8(e) false duty status, § 396.17(c) no valid annual inspection, § 396.3(a)(1) defects not corrected, § 392.4(a) driver with narcotics, § 392.82 hand-held phone use, § 172.504(a) improper hazmat placarding.
